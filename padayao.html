Welcome to Tuba!

Tuba is a hidden gem in the Philippines, where the cool mountain air and breathtaking landscapes create a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Its tranquil atmosphere, combined with its rich cultural heritage, offers visitors a refreshing retreat and a chance to reconnect with nature...

About Tuba

Tuba is a serene municipality nestled in the province of Benguet, Philippines, known for its picturesque landscapes and cool climate. It offers visitors a blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and exciting outdoor activities, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers....

Things to Do in Tuba

  • Reconnect with nature at Maryknoll Ecological Sanctuary
  • Take a dip at Asin Hot Springs
  • Explore culture and art at Tam-Awan Village
  • Aran Cave adventure
  • Visit the Baguio Cathedral of our lady of atonement
